Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:
Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the Focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra vergin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive-oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desidred, though it's not
    necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium sized-mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all
the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absored. You don't need to actually knead the
dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate
the last of the flour...
